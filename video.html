Pastor, Chaplain, Teacher

David J. Brett enjoys getting to speak and preach in such a dynamic setting as Village View Community Church. He speaks on rotation on Sunday Mornings and Wednesday evenings. Sunday mornings focus on biblical teaching in casual lecture style, whereas Wednesday evenings is a much more dynamic "flipped classroom" approach to Bible Study and theological training and teaching.
